How It Works

See our alert system in action to protect our fishermen

International Boundary

Advanced Boundary Detection

Our system uses GPS technology to monitor fishing boats in real-time. When a boat approaches within 50 nautical miles of an international maritime boundary, multiple alert systems are activated:

  • On-board siren and flashing lights
  • SMS alerts to the boat captain's phone
  • Notifications to coastal authorities
  • Alert messages to family members

This multi-layered approach ensures fishermen have ample warning to change course before accidentally crossing into foreign waters.
